Description

(?? !! Demo Only !! ??) Martial Arts are skills that randomly drop with weapons. The Martial Arts are tied to the weapon, and cannot be removed / changed. Lower tier weapons contain only 1 Martial Art, while higher tier weapons contain a maximum of 2 Martial Arts. Below contains weapon tables and the Martial Arts that can be applied.

Sword / Sabre Martial Arts

Martial Art Spirit Cost Weapon Type this Martial Art can be applied to Description
Increscent Slash
200 Sword Go into a stance by holding down the button, then let the button go to perform an attack. Releasing the at the right time will unleash a powerful attack.
Meteor Shower
280 Sword, Stright Sabre Attack with repeated thrusts.
Moon Break
360 Stright Sabre, Curved Sabre Slice through the ground upward, causing an energy wave to fly forward.
Moon Flight
120 Sword, Curved Sabre Swiftly swing your weapon upward and jump attack.
Moon Sweep
360 Sword, Straight Sabre, Curved Sabre Step forward while rotating the body to perform a frontward slash.
Star Fall
280 Curved Sabre Leap high and forcefully slash downward.
Sun Piercer
200 Sword Perform a fierce forward-facing thrust.

Martial Art Spirit Cost Description
