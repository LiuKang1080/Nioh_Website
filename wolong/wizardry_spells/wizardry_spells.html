Description

There are a total of: 30 Wizardry Spells (!! Only In Demo !!). There are 6 Wizardry Spells for each of the 5 Elemental Phases. Below are tables containing all of the spells along with the requirements to use those spells.

Five Phases Affinities

Wizardry Spells are based on the Five Phases theory in which each of the phases interacts with the others in certain ways. By understanding this, you can counter a Wizardry Spell itself, or the positive / negative effects caused by it.

"Wood overcomes Earth" . . . Wood Phase counters Earth Phase "Earth overcomes Water" . . . Earth Phase counters Water Phase "Water overcomes Fire" . . . Water Phase counters Fire Phase "Fire overcomes Metal" . . . Fire Phase counters Metal Phase "Metal overcomes Wood" . . . Metal Phase counters Wood Phase
