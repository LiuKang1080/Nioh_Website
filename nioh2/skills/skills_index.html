Description

There are 11 weapons in Nioh 2. Each weapon has its own Skill Tree containing new Skills and Passive Skills that are applied during combat. There are 2 support style Skill Trees in the form of Ninjutsu and Onmyo Magic. Finally there are 2 universal Skill Trees in the form of Samurai and Shiftling Skill Trees.

Checking proficiency Points: You can always check your current proficiency on Samurai, Shiftling, Onmyo, Ninjutsu, and Weapon Trees by: Pressing Start ⟶ Status ⟶ RB.
Acquiring Skill Points: You gain Weapon, Onmyo, and Ninjutsu Skill Points by reaching the next level in proficiency for that Weapon or Jutsu. You gain Weapon or Jutsu proficiency by using the Weapon or Jutsu in combat. Proficiency gained is based on the number of HITS, not the output of damage!
You can also unlock Skill Points by using Locks of Hair, these come in the form of: Samurai Locks of Hair, Specific Weapon Locks of Hair, and Ninjutsu / Onmyo Magic Locks of Hair. These Locks of Hair can be acquired as item pick ups in mission, or as mission rewards. Getting Shiftling Skill Points requires acquiring Soul Cores, and performing Resting Rites under the "Manage Soul Cores" section at the Shrine. Acquiring Samurai Skill Points requires mission progressions.

Maximum Shiftling Level: 90 - (59,000,000) Proficiency Points.
Maximum Ninjutsu & Onmyo Magic Level: 70 - (26,000,000) Proficiency Points.
Maximum Melee Weapon Level: 60 - (11,000,000) Proficiency Points.
