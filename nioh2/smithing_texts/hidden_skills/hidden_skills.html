Mechanics of "Art of Combat Skills" and "Hidden Skills"

Art of Combat / Hidden Skills behave the same as other Smithing Texts in terms of drop rate and drop mechanics. Rather than unlocking armor or weapon pieces they unlock hidden Skills for the Weapons, Ninjutsu, Onmyo, and Shiftling trees.
Farming Methods / Acquiring Hidden Skills: While the drop rate for Hidden Skills has greatly incresed compared to Nioh 1 we can still use certain buffs or builds designed to make farming these Hidden Skills easier.

How Luck affects Hidden Skills: Luck does NOT affect the drop rate of Smithing Texts or Hidden Skills. Luck only affects the rarity of items that drop, NOT the chance of an item dropping. Buffs like Luck Bringer Talisman, and Soul Purge Talisman does not affect the Smithing Text or Hidden Skills drop rate. Tempering Luck+ attributes on armor, accessories, and Soul Cores also does not affect the Smithing Texts / Hidden Skills drop rate.

How Item Drop Rate affects Hidden Skills: Item Drop Rate buffs DOES affect Smithing Text and Hidden Skills drop rate. There is no active buff that we can use to manually increase our IDR at will, but we can temper on the attribute "Increase Item Drop Rate" onto the Weapon & Armor pieces, Accessories, and Soul Cores.
